Insights

Unreal Engine Focus

The current industry shift highlights Unreal Engine's move beyond pure gaming into key domains like Virtual Production** for film and television, and Architectural Visualization. The power of Unreal Engine 5—specifically its Nanite (virtualized geometry) and Lumen (global illumination) systems—is setting a new standard for real-time fidelity, demanding developers acquire deep expertise in performance optimization for large-scale environments.


GAFX 2024 Takeaway

The Global Animation, VFX, Gaming, and Comics (GAFX) 2024 event underscored the rapid maturation of the Indian AVGC (Animation, Visual Effects, Gaming, and Comics) market. Key themes included the integration of Generative AI into asset creation pipelines to enhance speed, and a strong focus on talent development to meet global demands. The need for professionals who can bridge creative vision with technical execution (especially in real-time rendering) was a recurring highlight.


Workshop on Web development Takeaway

The Web Development Workshop provided an in-depth understanding of front-end and back-end technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks. It enhanced my practical skills in creating responsive, user-friendly websites and strengthened my knowledge of design principles, coding standards, and collaborative development workflows.
